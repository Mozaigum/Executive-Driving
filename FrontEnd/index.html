  • Standard detailing: $95–$150

  • Accepted methods: credit cards, e-Transfer, invoicing (approved), cash.

  • Identity & Contact: name, company, phone, email.
  • Trip Data: pickup/drop-off locations, dates/times, routing, instructions.
  • Communications: calls, emails, messages through apps or website.
  • Payment Data: tokenized card details (via payment processor); e-Transfer confirmations. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Device/App Telemetry: push tokens, crash logs, app diagnostics.
  • Safety & Compliance: incident reports, lost-and-found records, driver notes.

3) Purposes

We collect and use personal information to:

  • Accept, manage, and complete bookings.
  • Dispatch drivers and provide concierge support.

  • Improve our services, website, and app performance.
  • Send transactional notices; with your consent, send marketing communications under CASL (unsubscribe anytime).

4) Consent & Your Choices

We rely on your express or implied consent under PIPA for most collection and use of your personal information, along with other lawful grounds (fraud prevention, fee collection, legal claims). You may withdraw consent at any time, subject to legal and contractual limits with reasonable notice.

5) Sharing & Cross-Border Transfers

  • Payment processors: to handle payments and disputes.

Some service providers may store or process data outside Canada. This means it may be subject to foreign laws. We use contractual, organizational, and technical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information.

6) Security & Retention

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of your data. We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal and accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. Afterwards, we securely delete or de-identify the data.

7) Access, Correction & Complaints

You may request access to or correction of your personal information at any time. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Information and Privacy Commissioner (OIPC) of Alberta.

OIPC: 1-888-878-4044 — see “Request a Review / File a Complaint.”

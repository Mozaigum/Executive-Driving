Executive Driving Services — Terms & Conditions (Alberta)

Last updated: 11/25/2025

1) Who we are & Scope

These Terms & Conditions ("Terms") govern all executive/private SUV transportation and concierge services provided by Executive Driving Services ("EDS"). By placing a booking or using our services, you agree to these Terms.

2) Bookings, Changes & Substitutions

  • Accuracy: You must provide correct pickup times, locations, passenger count, luggage info, and any special requirements.
  • Verification: A pre-authorization may be placed before dispatch.
  • Vehicle Substitution: We may substitute a comparable or higher-class vehicle if required for safety or operational reasons, at no extra cost.
  • Delays Outside Our Control: Weather, traffic, road closures may affect ETA. We are not liable for indirect losses such as missed flights.

3) Rates, Waiting Time & Extras

  • Quoted rates include vehicle, chauffeur, and standard distance/time.
  • Waiting Time: 15-minute grace period, then billed in 15-minute increments.
  • Tolls/Parking: Charged at cost.
  • After-hours/peak surcharges may apply.

4) Cancellations & No-Shows

  • Sedan/SUV: Free cancel ≥ 24 hours; 50% charge 2–24 hours; 100% charge < 2 hours or no-show
  • Special Events: Free until 72 hours prior; 100% charge within 72 hours.

5) Passenger Conduct & Safety

  • Seatbelts mandatory for all passengers.
  • No smoking/vaping strictly prohibited.
  • No open liquor or cannabis consumption in vehicles.
  • Service animals allowed; pets must be pre-declared.

6) Cleaning, Damage & Downtime

  • Standard detailing: $95–$150
  • Spill/stain extraction: $150–$300
  • Biohazard cleanup: $300–$600 + downtime
  • Smoke/vape odour remediation: $250–$500

7) Payment & Chargebacks

  • Accepted: credit cards, e-Transfer, invoicing (approved), cash.
  • Overdue balances accrue interest at 1.5%/month.
  • Unjust chargebacks may incur a $200 admin fee plus legal/processor costs.

8) Non-Refundable Deposit

A 50% deposit is required to secure all bookings. This deposit is non-refundable at all times.

Executive Driving Services — Privacy Policy (Alberta PIPA)

Last updated: 11/25/2025

1) About this Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

2) What We Collect

  • Identity & Contact: name, company, phone, email.
  • Trip Data: pickup/drop-off locations, dates/times, routing, instructions.
  • Communications: calls, emails, messages through apps or website.
  • Payment Data: tokenized card details via payment processor. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Safety & Compliance: incident reports, lost-and-found records, driver notes.

3) Purposes

  • Accept, manage, and complete bookings.
  • Dispatch drivers and provide concierge support.
  • Verify identity, prevent fraud, and manage payments/chargebacks.
  • Ensure safety and comply with Alberta laws.
  • Send transactional notices; with consent, send marketing communications under CASL.

4) Consent & Your Choices

We rely on your express or implied consent under PIPA. You may withdraw consent at any time, subject to legal and contractual limits.

5) Security & Retention

We use appropriate safeguards and retain information only as long as necessary. Afterwards, we securely delete or de-identify the data.
